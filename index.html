What is Space?

Space in simple understanding is an area or volume.In science: Outer space, the area beyond the limit of the atmosphere of Earth. Space (physics), one of the fundamental concepts like time and mass. Space is the boundless three-dimensional extent in which objects and events have relative position and direction.
Physical space is often conceived in three linear dimensions, although modern physicists usually consider it, with time, to be part of a boundless four-dimensional continuum known as spacetime.
